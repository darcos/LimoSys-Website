Who are we?

Limosys Software was established in 1989, and it has been providing management software solutions specific to the ground transportation industry for over 30 years.

Read more...

Our mission is to provide ground transportation companies with cutting-edge technologies, guidance, leadership, and inventory sharing capabilities to effectively compete and thrive in this competitive marketplace.  Driven by our vast experience in every aspect and need of the ground transportation industry we have developed unique automation tools and workflow process designed to help any size company to increase productivity, reduce cost, boost sales and take control of their entire reservation, dispatch, billing and administration operation.
